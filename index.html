about us

How Flowers Make Your Everyday Life Colourful & Fragrant?

Our olfactory neurons play a large role in psychologically training us to feel differently in different situations. A sweet and soothing smell would rewind memories of things which are highly pleasurable. The bad smell would revive the painful incidents of life. Luckily, each flower comes up with a nice fragrance and whenever one crosses through such smell – the mind is automatically filled with happy feelings. Maybe someone is feeling low (due to millions of reasons) – but with just a whiff of a floral smell, there would be a change in behaviour.
